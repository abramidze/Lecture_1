This is page about Shrek

CHARACTERS :

SHREK

Shrek is a green humanoid ogre once living alone with a resentment towards those who judged him by appearance.

DONKEY

Donkey is a talkative, euphoric, happy-go-lucky, and flighty donkey.

LORD FARQUAAD

Lord Farquaad was the tyrannical and ruthless ruler of Duloc, who yearned for a "perfect world" free of all things magical.
